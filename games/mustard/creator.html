Attacker Modifiers


"Attack Power" is a multiplier to a skill's "Base Damage".
"Accuracy Mod" is a multiplier that's applied to the skill's "Accuracy". "Accuracy" is a % chance of the skill landing (e.g. 40 is a 40% chance). Same goes for a victim's "Dodge" and their chance of dodging the skill.
"Defense" is a multiplier applied to the total damage. (e.g. having "Defense" 0.5 would mean that a skill with a total damage of 10 would instead do 5 damage.)
